THE BEST MOVIE ACCORDING TO MAFARA

My Top3 movies of all time

Movie Name 1: Kuch Kuch Hota Hai

This movie is a about friendship and love.
Its about a friend who is in love with his bestfriend
but couldn't tell him because of his love for someone else.
The ending takes a beautiful turn. A must watch

Movie Name 2: Hera Pheri

This movie is comedy based on 3 friends or Tenants.
Its about the loan they had taken from people and
all the dramas they create to pay that.
Its fun and light hearted movie.
Recommended watching.

Movie Name 3: Veera Zaara

Again a masterpiece in its script as well as songs.
A beautiful tale of love between a hindu indian army boy and A muslim pakistani girl.
How their love story makes into beautiful ending is scenic and very emotional ride.
A Must Watch.
